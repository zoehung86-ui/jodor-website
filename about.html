About Jo D'or

Our Story

Jo D'or began the way most meaningful things do — quietly, after a season of becoming.

I spent years in the jewelry world before I ever made something of my own.

I trained at GIA, learned to read diamonds, and designed at a renowned Hollywood jewelry house — close to the makers and benches of Los Angeles' jewelry district.

I knew gold.
I knew gemstones.

But it took longer to know what I wanted to say with them.

Jo D'or is my answer.

I don't believe jewelry is a wish, or a way to manifest the life you want.

I believe it's proof.

Proof of a transformation that already happened.

The version of you who survived something.
Who grew quietly.
Who found her way back to herself.

That's who each piece is for.

Real gold and natural gemstones, shaped slowly and worn with intention.

Not loud.
Not trend.

Just yours.

Something to hold the moment you became who you are.

For the woman she's become
— Zoe, Founder & Designer

Jewelry isn't a wish for who you might become.
It is proof of who you already are.
