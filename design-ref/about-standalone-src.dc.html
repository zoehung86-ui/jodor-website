OUR STORY

I spent years in the jewelry world before I ever made something of my own.

I trained at GIA, learned to read diamonds, and designed at a renowned Hollywood jewelry house — close to the makers and benches of Los Angeles' jewelry district.

I knew gold. I knew gemstones.

But it took longer to know what I wanted to say with them.

Jo D'or is my answer.

I don't believe jewelry is a wish, or a way to manifest the life you want. I believe it's a testament. Validation of a transformation that already happened.

The version of you who survived something.
Who grew quietly.
Who found her way back to herself.

Each piece is crafted and shaped with intention, using real gold and natural gemstones.

Not loud. Not trendy. Just you.

A piece symbolizing the moment you became the person you are today.
